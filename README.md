Simplified, Streamlined, ready for deployment.
=========
all edits to code were done in a codespace with an agent. any errors and building failures have been triple checked and fixed by said agent as of writing this. open to further fixes and streamlining without compromising functionality. definitely feel free to fork and improve upon the edits I made for your own use cases. for me I just wanted a small scale fanfiction archive platform with tagging, that didn't allow "legally gray" CSAM by admins of the site.

Problems with AO3 in particular
=========
warning for non explicit CSA: [I am Fictional? by JaneMardill448](https://archiveofourown.org/works/73432101)

I've always been a critic of bigotry and abuse in fandom spaces, and the morally bankrupt moderation and "legal practices" of OTW really just deadens the soul. I honest to god cannot imagine being a child and stumbling across RPF porn of myself on that site, emailing them to report it only to be told that **there's nothing legally or morally wrong with it and they won't take it down**. I also cannot imagine being the one to tell that child that literal written CSAM of them is fictional and thus protected by their team of lawyers, with a straight face. I cannot imagine being that person and also sleeping well at night. I just can't. 

fandom should not be a safe, lawless place for pedophiles, or racists for that matter. fandom should be accessible and welcoming to everybody, yes. but the moment you prioritize the "freedom" of members writing RPF CSAM or violent racist hatespeech, you are saying that their comfort matters more than the people affected by their writing. you might not be willing to do something to extend a safespace for victims of pedophilia or racism **but I am**. 

that is the reason why I want to simplify AO3 and make it ready for one click deployment. you shouldn't have to spend hours upon hours figuring out how to set up a MySQL database, or how to spin up a server on Ubuntu or CentOS just for a place to put your fanfiction and other fanworks that isn't moderated by ghouls.

Future Goals
=========
I want to eventually unlink this archive from the original and go in my own developmental direction, as well as utilize it to create my own site. I already have a domain ready, I just need to customize and deploy.
I plan to include by default some additions from the community that help with accessibility, like a dyslexia friendly font, highlighted tags, better icons, and the like. dark mode will be the default skin, not light mode, as well. the TOS and guidelines will be reworked to be more in line with my personal morals, and as the site scales I will vet volunteers and have heavier moderation for bad actors. I do not prioritize "free speech" that makes allowances for illegal and harmful content, nor hate speech. hate speech is not free speech to me.

those that don't care, feel free to fork with your own rendition. or continue to use ao3, or perhaps squidgeworld, I'm not your keeper. those that do, I hope to see us collaborate in the future and build something that lets the marginalized, silenced, and overlooked fandom population a place to thrive too.
